Core capabilities

CNC machining

4-axis milling in aluminum, stainless, brass, titanium, and engineering plastics. Setups stay ready for quick reruns.

  • Prototyping and production runs
  • Custom Fixturing
  • Surface finishing through local partners

CNC turning

Lathe Turning for bushings, shafts, and housings with hand-fit checks before shipping.

  • Brass, Aluminum, Delrin & More
  • Threading, grooving, and parting tools on-hand
  • From 5 parts to 500+

3D printing

Carbon-filled nylon and engineering-grade plastics for tooling, fixtures, and workholding.

  • Printer-ready in hours for fixtures or quick parts
  • Hybrid Carbon Nylon prints + machined plates for rigid tooling
  • Bulk PA6-CF Parts

Software Development

Lightweight firmware, scripts, and automation to get business problems solved fast.

  • Inventory management + e-commerce
  • Data logging or control scripts for test rigs
  • Apps to link tooling, machining, and reporting

Instant quoting built for prototypes

Upload CAD, prints, or even a napkin sketch. Our quoting flow calls out risks, suggests materials, and books the next open slot on the mill or lathe.

  • Supports STEP, Parasolid, Fusion 360, and SolidWorks files
  • Material and finish suggestions baked in
  • Secure uploads with quick human follow-up if anything looks off
