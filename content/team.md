## Our Team

<div class="team-grid">

<div class="team-member">

<h3>Avery Wagar</h3>
<p class="team-role">Founder & CEO</p>
<p>Avery founded Future Present Labs with a vision to bridge the gap between software and physical manufacturing. With a background in systems engineering and a passion for building, he leads the company's strategic direction and technical vision. Avery is obsessed with making American manufacturing competitive again through automation, AI, and modern tooling.</p>
</div>

<div class="team-member">
<img src="/images/team/james.jpg" alt="James Merrill" class="team-photo">
<h3>James Merrill</h3>
<p class="team-role">Chief Operating Officer</p>
<p>James runs operations at Future Present Labs, overseeing system design and new integrations. A University of Washington graduate with a background in real estate and business, James brings an entrepreneurial mindset honed through ventures in luxury events and caviar. He ensures the team's ambitious technical projects translate into smooth operational reality.</p>
</div>

<div class="team-member">
<img src="/images/team/nick.jpg" alt="Nick Kust" class="team-photo">
<h3>Nick Kust</h3>
<p class="team-role">Head of Product</p>
<p>Nick leads product strategy and development at Future Present Labs. Born in Moscow and educated at the University of Washington (Economics, Class of 2025), he brings a global perspective to building products at the intersection of software and hardware. Nick focuses on translating complex technical concepts into intuitive user experiences.</p>
