## Our Team

<div class="team-grid">

<div class="team-member">
<img src="/images/team/avery.jpg" alt="Avery Wagar" class="team-photo">
<h3>Avery Wagar</h3>
<p class="team-role">Founder & CEO</p>
<p>Avery founded Future Present Labs with a vision to bridge the gap between software and physical manufacturing. With a background in systems engineering and a passion for building, he leads the company's strategic direction and technical vision. Avery is focused on making American manufacturing competitive through automation, AI, and modern tooling.</p>
</div>

<div class="team-member">
<img src="/images/team/james.jpg" alt="James Merrill" class="team-photo">
<h3>James Merrill</h3>
<p class="team-role">Chief Operating Officer</p>
<p>James runs operations at Future Present Labs, overseeing system design and new integrations. A University of Washington graduate with a background in real estate and business, James brings an entrepreneurial mindset to building efficient manufacturing operations. He ensures the team's ambitious technical projects translate into smooth operational reality, from production scheduling to client delivery.</p>
</div>

<div class="team-member">
<img src="/images/team/nick.jpg" alt="Nick Kust" class="team-photo">
<h3>Nick Kust</h3>
<p class="team-role">Head of Product</p>
<p>Nick leads product at Future Present Labs, shaping the tools and systems that power agentic manufacturing. Born in Moscow and educated at the University of Washington (Economics, Class of 2025), he brings a global perspective to building product infrastructure for modern fabrication workflows. Nick focuses on bridging the gap between complex manufacturing processes and intuitive user experiences.</p>
</div>
